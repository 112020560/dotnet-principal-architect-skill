# Architecture Scorecard

> Summarize the architectural health of a software system by consolidating the evidence produced during the Architecture Review into an executive and technical assessment.

---

# Purpose

The Architecture Scorecard provides a concise assessment of the software architecture.

Its objectives are to:

- Summarize the Architecture Review.
- Measure architectural health.
- Highlight architectural strengths.
- Identify architectural risks.
- Prioritize architectural improvements.
- Assess long-term maintainability.
- Support executive decision-making.

This scorecard does not perform an architecture review.

It summarizes the engineering evidence produced by the Architecture Review.
