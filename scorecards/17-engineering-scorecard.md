# Engineering Excellence Scorecard

> Consolidate engineering evidence from all engineering reviews into a single executive view that measures the overall health, maturity, and production confidence of a software system.

---

# Purpose

The Engineering Excellence Scorecard provides an executive summary of the engineering health of a software system.

Its objectives are to:

- Consolidate engineering review results.
- Measure engineering capability.
- Highlight organizational risks.
- Identify technical investment priorities.
- Provide executive visibility.
- Support production readiness decisions.
- Track engineering excellence over time.

This scorecard summarizes engineering evidence.

It does not perform engineering reviews.

All engineering evidence originates from the Engineering Review Framework.
