# Testing Scorecard

> Summarize the maturity, effectiveness, and confidence of the software testing strategy by consolidating the evidence produced during the Testing Review.

---

# Purpose

The Testing Scorecard provides an executive and technical assessment of the software testing strategy.

Its objectives are to:

- Summarize the Testing Review.
- Measure testing maturity.
- Assess software quality confidence.
- Identify testing risks.
- Prioritize quality improvements.
- Evaluate test automation.
- Support production readiness.

This scorecard does not perform a testing review.

It summarizes the engineering evidence produced by the Testing Review.
