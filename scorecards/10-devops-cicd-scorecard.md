# DevOps & CI/CD Scorecard

> Summarize the maturity, reliability, and automation of the software delivery platform by consolidating the evidence produced during the DevOps & CI/CD Review.

---

# Purpose

The DevOps & CI/CD Scorecard provides an executive and technical assessment of the software delivery lifecycle.

Its objectives are to:

- Summarize the DevOps & CI/CD Review.
- Measure delivery maturity.
- Assess deployment reliability.
- Identify delivery risks.
- Prioritize DevOps improvements.
- Evaluate release automation.
- Support continuous delivery excellence.

This scorecard does not perform a DevOps review.

It summarizes the engineering evidence produced by the DevOps & CI/CD Review.
