# Production Readiness Scorecard

> Summarize the operational readiness of the software system for production by consolidating the evidence produced across all engineering reviews.

---

# Purpose

The Production Readiness Scorecard provides an executive assessment of the overall production readiness of a software system.

Its objectives are to:

- Consolidate all engineering review results.
- Measure production readiness.
- Assess operational confidence.
- Identify remaining production risks.
- Prioritize final engineering improvements.
- Support production deployment decisions.

This scorecard does not perform any engineering review.

It summarizes the evidence produced across all Engineering Reviews.
