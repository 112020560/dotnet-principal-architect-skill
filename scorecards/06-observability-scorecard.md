# Observability Scorecard

> Summarize the observability maturity of a software system by consolidating the evidence produced during the Observability Review.

---

# Purpose

The Observability Scorecard provides an executive and technical assessment of the system's ability to be monitored, diagnosed, and operated effectively in production.

Its objectives are to:

- Summarize the Observability Review.
- Measure observability maturity.
- Assess operational visibility.
- Identify monitoring gaps.
- Prioritize operational improvements.
- Evaluate incident readiness.
- Support production excellence.

This scorecard does not perform an observability review.

It summarizes the engineering evidence produced by the Observability Review.
