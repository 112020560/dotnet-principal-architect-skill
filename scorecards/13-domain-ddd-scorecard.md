# Domain & DDD Scorecard

> Summarize the quality, maturity, and effectiveness of the domain model by consolidating the evidence produced during the Domain & DDD Review.

---

# Purpose

The Domain & DDD Scorecard provides an executive and technical assessment of the business domain model.

Its objectives are to:

- Summarize the Domain & DDD Review.
- Measure domain model maturity.
- Assess business alignment.
- Identify domain modeling risks.
- Prioritize domain improvements.
- Evaluate Domain-Driven Design practices.
- Support long-term business evolution.

This scorecard does not perform a Domain & DDD review.

It summarizes the engineering evidence produced by the Domain & DDD Review.
