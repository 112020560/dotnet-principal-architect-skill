# Engineering Governance Scorecard

> Summarize the maturity, consistency, and effectiveness of engineering governance practices by consolidating the evidence produced during the Engineering Governance Review.

---

# Purpose

The Engineering Governance Scorecard provides an executive and technical assessment of the engineering practices, standards, and governance model used throughout the software development lifecycle.

Its objectives are to:

- Summarize the Engineering Governance Review.
- Measure engineering governance maturity.
- Assess organizational engineering discipline.
- Identify governance risks.
- Prioritize governance improvements.
- Evaluate engineering standards adoption.
- Support sustainable engineering excellence.

This scorecard does not perform an Engineering Governance review.

It summarizes the engineering evidence produced by the Engineering Governance Review.
