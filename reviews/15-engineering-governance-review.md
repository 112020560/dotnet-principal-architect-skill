# Engineering Governance Compliance Review

> Evaluate whether the organization has the engineering governance necessary to consistently produce, maintain, and evolve high-quality software independent of individual contributors.

---

# Purpose

This review evaluates the organization's engineering governance and its ability to sustain software excellence over time.

Its objectives are to:

- Verify compliance with the Engineering Standard.
- Evaluate engineering governance maturity.
- Assess architecture governance practices.
- Review engineering decision processes.
- Evaluate knowledge management.
- Assess technical debt governance.
- Measure long-term engineering sustainability.
- Capture organizational engineering risks.

This review verifies compliance.

It does not redefine engineering rules.

All referenced rules are defined in:

> docs/00-rule-catalog.md
