# Engineering Excellence Certification

> Formally recognize that a software system and its engineering organization have demonstrated exceptional engineering maturity, quality, and operational excellence.

---

## Purpose

The Engineering Excellence Certification formally declares that a software system has achieved a level of engineering maturity, quality, and operational capability that meets the highest standards defined by the Engineering Assessment Framework.

This certification is awarded to systems that demonstrate engineering excellence across all engineering domains, not merely acceptable compliance.

This certification does not perform engineering analysis.

This certification does not issue recommendations.

All engineering evidence originates from the Engineering Maturity Assessment and the Engineering Excellence Scorecard.

## Validity

This certification is valid for the system version and organization identified above.

Re-certification is required when:

- The overall engineering grade declines below the certification threshold.
- A new engineering maturity assessment identifies significant regression.
- The certification period expires.
- Major architectural or organizational changes occur.
